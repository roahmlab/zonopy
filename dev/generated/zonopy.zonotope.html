1D Zonotope class

A Zonotope is a set of the form

Z={c+∑i=1Naigi  |  ai∈[−1,1]}\mathcal{Z} = \left\{c + \sum_{i=1}^{N} a_i g_i \; \middle\vert \; a_i \in [-1,1]\right\} Z={c+i=1∑N​ai​gi​​ai​∈[−1,1]}

where ccc is the center and gig_igi​ are the generators, each of which is a vector in the same space as ccc.

This class defines a basic zonotope and its operations. Z\mathbf{Z}Z is a tensor of shape (N+1)×d(N+1) \times d(N+1)×d where NNN is the number of generators and ddd is the dimension of the zonotope. The first row of ZZZ is the center and the rest are the generators, such that Z=[c,G]T\mathbf{Z} = [c,G]^TZ=[c,G]T. G=[g1,g2,…,gN]G = [g_1, g_2, \ldots, g_N]G=[g1​,g2​,…,gN​] is a matrix of shape N×dN \times dN×d.
